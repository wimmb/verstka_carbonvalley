About Us

A small team with big ideas and a 3D printer that never sleeps

Carbon Valley was born the way most good things are — from a genuine passion for cars and a desire to do something better than what was already out there. What started as a hobby project in Wheeling, Illinois, slowly turned into a real business, driven by one simple idea: even the smallest detail on your vehicle should reflect who you are.

We design and produce custom license plate frames for drivers, businesses, and dealerships across the United States. Our frames are cast from high-quality plastic built to handle daily road conditions — UV exposure, temperature changes, car washes — without losing shape or finish. Every frame is made to last, not just to look good on day one.

What sets our frames apart is how we approach customization. The text, logos, and graphics on each frame are created using 3D printing technology, which gives us a level of detail and precision that standard engraving or sticker methods simply can't match. Whether it's a single line of text or a full business logo, the result is clean, sharp, and built into the frame itself.

Our LED frames take things a step further. Each illuminated frame comes with built-in overvoltage and reverse polarity protection, so installation is safe and straightforward. Brightness is fully adjustable, giving you control over how subtle or bold the effect looks — day or night. It's the kind of detail that matters when you're putting something on your car.

We've worked with individual drivers, small businesses, and corporate clients including auto dealerships who needed a consistent, professional look across their fleet. Every order — whether it's one frame or a hundred — gets the same attention. No shortcuts, no mass-production mentality. Just well-made frames from a small team that genuinely cares about the craft.

We're based in Wheeling, Illinois, and ship across all 50 states. If you're looking for something standard, something fully custom, or something for your entire business — we're here to make it happen.
